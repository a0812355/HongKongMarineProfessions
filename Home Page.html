Our Goal

To protect the Ocean, April 2, 2024

“With every drop of water you drink, every breath you take, you’re connected to the sea.”

— Sylvia Earle

The ocean plays a crucial role in human life and ocean pollution become a severe global challenge, not only through its significant function in climate regulation, helping to mitigate the effects of climate change, but also as a vital source of food resources. By ensuring the sustainability of these resources, the ocean supports the nourishment and livelihood of countless communities. Furthermore, the global economy is deeply interconnected with the ocean's bounty, with industries such as fishing, tourism, and shipping being fundamental to the economic well-being of millions of people around the world.

Marine professionals work tirelessly behind the scenes, making significant contributions to the protection of marine resources and the maintenance of ecological balance. Their efforts have a profound impact on our lives, ensuring the health of our oceans and, by extension, the wellbeing of our planet. Despite their crucial role, many people remain unaware of the specialists in marine fields and the vital work they perform, which is essential for both the environment and our continued prosperity.
